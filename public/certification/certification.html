Certification Prerequisites

The IBM Open Platform for Apache Spark Partner certification offering has the following prerequisites

  1. The certification must be performed on the IBM Open Platform for non-production environment native installation available from the IBM Analytics Download Hadoop trials page.
  2. Partner application must be Apache Sparks 1.6.1 compliant or above
  3. The Partner application must execute in a Yarn deployment model only i.e. cannot be tested in a standalone deployment model or configured on Mesos

Apache Spark Partner Community

A premium Partner enablement engineer will be available to support the IOP installation process, if necessary, once all IBM Open Platform prerequisites are satisfied.

IBM will advertise the Partner commercial logotype on www.ibm.com/spark web site upon the completion and review of the Apache Spark Partner certification using the IBM Open Platform.

Once the Partner application is deemed Apache Spark Certified on the IBM Open Platform, the Partner agrees to the following:

  1. The Partner commits to re-certify in a timely fashion on subsequent releases of IBM Open Platform which will include major revisions to Apache Spark ecosystem component.
  2. Promote IBM Open Source Analytics Ecosystem logotype on their website.
